About

I am an amateur astronomer focused on photometry. I actively contribute exoplanet transit observations to the ExoClock project, as well as to the ETD and AAVSO databases. Every now and then, I participate in AAVSO variable star observing campaigns, report asteroid and comet astrometry to the Minor Planet Center, and collaborate in occultation observation campaigns.

Contact

If you are involved in an astronomy research project, observing campaign, or citizen science initiative and think my observations could be of value, I would be happy to hear from you. Feel free to get in touch to discuss how I might contribute. ✍️ hola@joaquin.ar
